Government Program Qualification

üéØ Investment Tax Credit (30%)

How It Works:

Dollar-for-dollar federal tax credit. Can be monetized immediately by selling to tax equity investors for $0.85-$0.90 per dollar.

Qualification:
  • Business entity ownership (LLC, Corp, Partnership)
  • Used for business purposes (>50% business use)
  • System placed in service by 2032
Benefit: $360,000 immediate cash

üìà MACRS Depreciation

How It Works:

5-year accelerated depreciation. 2025: 40% bonus depreciation in Year 1. Depreciable basis = 85% of cost (reduced by half of ITC).

Qualification:
  • Same as ITC requirements
  • Used for income-generating activities
  • Must own equipment (no leasing)
Benefit: $235,000 in tax savings over 5 years

üåæ USDA REAP Grant (50%)

How It Works:

Direct grant, no repayment. Competitive application with quarterly deadlines. Can combine with ITC and MACRS.

Qualification:
  • Rural location (population <50,000)
  • Agricultural producer (50%+ ag income) OR rural small business
  • 51%+ business electricity use
  • No outstanding federal debts
Benefit: Up to $600,000 direct grant
